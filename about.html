Welcome to ABSI

Access Business Solutions International is a business intelligence company delivering tailored corporate conferences and trainings based on the latest market research and emerging issues in the relevant industry.

We integrate cutting edge technologies and present-day global business trends in our products which make us the preferred option for companies seeking sustainable growth and profit through the adoption of effective corporate strategies.

We aim to provide creative B2B solutions for local and international enterprises through the promotion of global opportunities for our clients to profit from, through interaction and learning.

Strategic Partnerships

We partner with globally renowned companies and individuals in their respective fields. This, coupled with our own dedicated team of highly driven and exceptionally experienced staff makes us more than capable of delivering cutting edge business solutions.

Our Mission

To provide our partners and clients with a platform to generate strategic business intelligence through interaction and learning.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in the provision of relevant, strategic, and current day business information that gives our clients the competitive advantage

Our Values

We actively seek and encourage innovation by our staff, clients and partners alike.

  • We strive to provide strategic business intelligence to all our clients through our research-driven events.
  • We strive to adhere to strict professional standards and adopt an ‘above board’ approach to everything we do
  • We strive to nurture and develop mutually beneficial and sustained relationships with our partners and clients.
  • We employ HR best practices to keep our dedicated team of highly qualified professionals happy and motivated.
  • We strive to make a valuable contribution to society through our Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives.
